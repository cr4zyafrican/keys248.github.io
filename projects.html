Hunt for the World's Best Views

Around 2012 I started an effort to capture landscape views from various hikes and travels. Most of these photos come from hikes in California or my time in New York. Other photos are from travels around the U.S. and the world. More photos can be found on my tumblr blog at best-views.tumblr.com.
